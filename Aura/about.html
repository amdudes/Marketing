The Origin

Born from
Frustration.

AURA was founded in 2019 by Arjun Mehta after watching too many premium brands settle for mediocre social media. Brands with beautiful products, compelling stories, and real potential — trapped inside generic content and hollow strategies.

We built AURA to be the agency we wished existed — one that treats every brand like a luxury house, every campaign like an art direction brief, and every client's rupee like it's our own.

Today we're a 40-person studio across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore. We've elevated 250+ brands, generated 18M+ followers, and managed over ₹120Cr in ad spend. And we're just warming up.
