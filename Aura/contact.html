Questions Answered

Common
Questions.

How quickly can you begin?
We can onboard a new client and launch within 7–10 business days. Audit begins immediately on signing. Content creation starts in week 2.
Do you work with emerging brands?
Absolutely. Our Maison package is designed for brands at the beginning of their growth journey. What matters is your ambition, not your current size.
How do you define and measure success?
Based on your business goals — not vanity metrics. Whether ROAS, leads, revenue, or growth, we set KPIs upfront and report against them weekly.
What is the minimum engagement term?
We work on a minimum 3-month engagement. Social media compounds over time. After 3 months, we move to month-to-month. Most clients stay 12+ months.
Is ad spend included in the retainer?
No. Our retainer covers strategy and execution. Ad spend goes directly to the platforms at cost — no markup. Full transparency, always.
Do I get access to live performance data?
Atelier and Couture clients receive a live dashboard. All clients receive weekly recaps and a comprehensive monthly strategy report.
