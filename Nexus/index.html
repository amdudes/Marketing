FAQ

Questions?
Answered.

Everything you need to know before partnering with Nexus Social.

What makes Nexus Social different from other agencies?
We combine deep creative talent with data science. Every decision we make is backed by performance data, not intuition. Our team has managed $48M+ in ad spend and our average client ROAS is 4.8x — well above industry benchmarks.
How long until I see real results?
Organic growth shows meaningful momentum within 60–90 days. Paid campaigns typically deliver measurable ROI within the first 30 days. We set clear expectations in our strategy call based on your specific goals and starting point.
Do you work with small businesses or only large brands?
We work with ambitious brands at every stage — from funded startups to 8-figure companies. What matters is your growth mindset, not your current size. Our tiered packages accommodate different budgets while maintaining our standard of excellence.
Which platforms do you specialize in?
We specialize across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, and Pinterest. Our recommendation is always platform-specific and based on where your audience actually lives — not where it's trendy to be.
Do you handle creative content or just strategy?
Both. We have a full in-house creative studio — designers, videographers, copywriters, and motion artists. You can hand us the keys completely, or collaborate as much as you want. We adapt to your workflow.
What does onboarding look like?
After signing, we kick off with a comprehensive discovery session, brand immersion, and competitor deep-dive. Within 2 weeks, you'll have a full strategy document, content calendar, and campaign blueprints ready for approval.
