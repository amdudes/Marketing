Our Origin

Built by Marketers,
for Brands That Refuse to Settle

Nexus Social was born out of frustration. Our founders — veterans from Google, Meta's internal marketing teams, and top-tier creative agencies — watched too many great brands get mediocre results from agencies that prioritized vanity metrics over actual business growth.

In 2019, we set out to build something different: an agency that combines the creative soul of a boutique studio with the analytical rigor of a performance marketing firm. No smoke and mirrors — just strategies that scale revenue.

Today, we're a team of 40+ specialists spanning strategy, creative, paid media, data analytics, and community management. Our clients range from category-defining DTC startups to publicly traded corporations — all united by their ambition to lead their market on social.

"We don't measure success in followers or impressions. We measure it in revenue generated, market share captured, and businesses transformed. That's the Nexus standard."

— Daniel Okafor, Co-Founder & CEO
