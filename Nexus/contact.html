Before You Reach Out

Common Questions

What happens after I submit the contact form?
A senior Nexus Social team member reviews your submission and reaches out within 24 hours (usually much faster). We'll schedule a complimentary 45-minute strategy call where we discuss your brand, goals, and what's realistically achievable. No pitch deck, no hard sell — just an honest conversation.
Is the strategy call really free? What's the catch?
100% free, no catch. We invest in the strategy call because we only partner with brands we're genuinely confident we can help. The session gives us both the information to decide if we're the right fit — and gives you real value regardless of whether we work together.
What's the minimum budget to work with Nexus Social?
Our retained management packages start at $1,500/month (strategy + management, no ad spend). For paid media management, we recommend a minimum of $3,000/month in ad spend to generate statistically meaningful data. We'll always tell you honestly if your budget is below what's needed to achieve your goals.
Do you work with brands outside the US?
Absolutely. We have clients in 18+ countries across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Our London and Dubai offices give us native market intelligence for international campaigns, and we've built teams fluent in multiple languages and cultures.
How long are your contracts?
We offer both 3-month minimum agreements and month-to-month arrangements (at a slight premium). We don't believe in trapping clients — we believe in delivering results that make them want to stay. Our average client tenure is 18+ months.
