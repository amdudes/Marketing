How quickly will I see results from digital marketing?
Results vary by channel. PPC campaigns can show results within days, while SEO typically takes 3–6 months to see significant gains. Social media and content marketing generally show meaningful traction within 60–90 days with consistent effort.
What makes Marko different from other agencies?
We combine data-driven strategy with genuine creative thinking. Unlike agencies that apply cookie-cutter templates, we build fully custom strategies for each client. You get a dedicated account manager, transparent weekly reporting, and direct access to specialists.
Do you work with small businesses?
Absolutely. We have pricing plans starting at $499/month designed specifically for small businesses. We've helped hundreds of small businesses achieve impressive growth, and we scale our services to match your budget and goals.
How do I get started with Marko?
Simply fill out the contact form above or call us directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute strategy consultation to understand your goals, audit your current digital presence, and recommend the best plan for your business.
Are there long-term contracts?
We offer both monthly and longer-term contracts. While we recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to see meaningful results, we don't lock you into long-term agreements. Our goal is to earn your continued business through results.
What does your reporting process look like?
All clients receive detailed performance reports — monthly for Starter, weekly for Growth, and a live dashboard for Enterprise. Reports cover all KPIs, campaign performance, ROI, and upcoming strategic recommendations. We also hold monthly strategy calls.
