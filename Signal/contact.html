Common Questions

FAQ

How quickly can you start?
We can typically onboard a new client and launch within 7-10 business days. After signing, we begin the brand audit and strategy blueprint immediately. Content creation starts in week 2.
Do you work with small businesses?
Absolutely. Our Starter package is designed specifically for small businesses and startups. We've scaled brands from zero followers and minimal budgets. What matters is your ambition, not your current size.
How do you measure success?
We define success based on your business goals — not vanity metrics. Whether that's ROAS, leads generated, follower growth, or revenue, we set KPIs upfront and report against them every week.
What's the minimum contract length?
We work on a minimum 3-month engagement because social media growth takes time to compound. After 3 months, we move to month-to-month. Most clients stay with us for 12+ months because they see results.
Do you handle ad spend separately?
Yes. Our management fee covers strategy and execution. Ad spend is a separate budget that goes directly to the platforms (Meta, Google, etc.) and is billed at cost with no markup. Full transparency.
Can I see my campaign data in real time?
Yes. Growth and Dominate package clients get access to a live dashboard. All clients receive weekly performance recaps and a comprehensive monthly report with recommendations.
